About Moon Public School

Moon Public School is an educational institution functioning under the esteemed Karuna Vanni Charitable and Educational Trust, an organization dedicated to the promotion and advancement of education and social welfare. Established with the vision of nurturing young minds, the school focuses on providing quality education that blends academic excellence with moral values and holistic development. The trust, founded with the objective of contributing to society through education and charitable initiatives, ensures that Moon Public School operates with a strong foundation of integrity, discipline, and inclusivity. The institution strives to create a supportive and inspiring learning environment where students are encouraged to explore their potential, develop critical thinking, and grow into responsible citizens. Through its commitment to educational excellence and community development, Moon Public School reflects the core mission of the trust—empowering individuals through knowledge and fostering a better future for society.

Our Cause

At Moon Public School, under the Karuna Vanni Charitable and Educational Trust, we believe that education is not a privilege—it is a right every child deserves. Our mission is to provide quality education to students from all backgrounds, especially those who lack access to proper learning opportunities due to financial limitations. Many talented and hardworking children are forced to leave their education midway because of economic challenges. Through your support, we aim to bridge this gap by offering affordable education, better infrastructure, learning resources, and a safe environment where every child can grow with dignity and confidence. Your contribution helps us: Provide scholarships to underprivileged students Improve classrooms, libraries, and digital learning facilities Support educational materials, uniforms, and basic needs Create opportunities for holistic development beyond academics Every donation, big or small, brings us one step closer to shaping a brighter future for these children. Together, we can empower young minds, uplift communities, and build a society driven by knowledge and compassion. Be a part of this change. Support education. Support the future.

About the Trust

Karuna Vanni Charitable and Educational Trust is a non-profit organization committed to creating a positive impact in society through education and charitable initiatives. Established with the vision of empowering individuals and uplifting communities, the trust works towards providing accessible and quality education, especially to those in need. The foundation of the trust lies in the values of compassion, integrity, and service. It strives to support underprivileged sections of society by promoting educational opportunities, social welfare programs, and community development initiatives. Through its initiatives, including the management of Moon Public School, the trust focuses on nurturing young minds, encouraging lifelong learning, and building a strong moral foundation in students. The trust believes that education is the key to transformation and works continuously to make it accessible, inclusive, and impactful. With a commitment to long-term social change, Karuna Vanni Charitable and Educational Trust aims to contribute towards a better, more educated, and empowered society.
